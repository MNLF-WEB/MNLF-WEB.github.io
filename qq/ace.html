Ace Spectrum

Did you know that you don't have to love anyone?

Asexual

A person who does not exhibit sexual attraction.

Aromantic

A person who does not exhibit romantic attraction.

Minor terms

These terms, while less common, do come up from time to time

Greysexual

A person who exhibits minimal sexual attraction.

Greyromantic

A person who exhibits minimal romantic attraction.

Demisexual

A person who, in order for a sexual attraction to occur, must have formed emotional bonds with a person.
