What do they mean by 'Cis'?

Heterosexual

Sexually attracted to someone of a different gender.

Heteroromantic

Romantically attracted to someone of a different gender.

Cisgender

A person whose gender identity is that of which they were ascribed at birth.

Minor terms

These terms, while less common, do come up from time to time

Allosexual

People who exhibit sexual attraction to others.

Alloromantic

People who exhibit romantic attraction to others.

Heteroflexible

Attracted to someone of a different gender, but may occasionally have attractions to those of the same gender.
