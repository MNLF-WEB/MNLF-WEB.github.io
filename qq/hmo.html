Gay Spectrum

Did you know that two dudes can love eachother?

Gay

(of a man) Sexually Attracted to men.

Lesbian

(of a woman) Sexually Attracted to women.

Homosexual

Sexually attracted to someone of the same gender.

Homoromantic

Romantically attracted to someone of the same gender.

Minor terms

These terms, while less common, do come up from time to time

Sapphic

(of a woman) Romantically attracted to women.

Achillean

(of a man) Romantically Attracted to men.

Homoflexible

Attracted to someone of the same gender, but may occasionally have attractions to those of a different gender

Gynosexual

Sexually attracted to women or womanliken.

Androsexual

Sexually attracted to men or manliken.
