Bi Spectrum

Did you know that you don't have to love just men or women?

Bisexual

Attracted sexually to people of the same gender and different genders.

Biromantic

Attracted romantically to people of the same gender and different genders.

Minor terms

These terms, while less common, do come up from time to time

Pansexual

Attracted sexually to people of any gender.

Polysexual

Attracted sexually to people of multiple genders.

Polyamourous

A person with an interest in romantic ettaut sexual relations with multiple people.
